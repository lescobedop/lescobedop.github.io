Welcome to my website! My name is Lucho Escobedo, and I'm an experienced data scientist ready to applied my expertise anywhere in the world starting on June 2020.

**Here are 10 cool facts about me:**

1. All my degrees are in **Geography**. My doctoral dissertation focuses on methods for detecting **industrial clusters**, understanding **retail location decisions**, and assessing substitutability of **consumer packaged goods** for retailers in the pharmaceutical industry.

2. I speak **Spanish**, **Portuguese** and **English** with the same level of proficiency.

3. After graduation I worked as an **applied economics researcher** in the **Group for the Analysis of Development**, one of the top Think Tanks in Latin America.

4. My alma mater is the **University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign**.

5. I am a **musician**, and recorded an album with my band in 2010. I play the drums, but also like playing the guitar and the ukulele.

6. During my graduate education I was awarded with the **Fulbright Scholarship** and the **Tinker Fellowship**.

7. I was honoured to served as the **President of the Association of Peruvian Students** at the University of Illinois for 2 terms.

8. My first experience as an **analytics consultant** was at the Center for Innovation in Teaching & Learning in 2014.

9. In the Fall of 2017 I accepted an offer extended by **McKinsey & Company** to join their **Data Science** team in Latin America, and move to Sao Paulo, Brazil.

10. I recently took a **sabbatical**. My wife and 4 year-old son lived in Central Illinois while I was on Brazil. Being apart was very hard, but I'm currently fully focused on them.
