Hi, I'm Lucho Escobedo, a passionate leader and data expert. As Head of Data at ADP, I drive data-driven initiatives to optimize operations, enhance customer experiences, and boost revenue. My work has generated millions in value, showcasing the transformative power of strategic data leadership.

Beyond my corporate role, I’m an entrepreneur and investor, leading Green Street Holdings. We’re a firm empowering exceptional businesses and entrepreneurs. By providing tailored partnerships and value creation, we identify and support promising companies with strong fundamentals and high-growth potential. 

With over 15 years of experience at the intersection of technology and management consulting, I bring a unique blend of data expertise, business strategy, and leadership to every endeavor. I've held impactful roles at McKinsey & Company and REEF Technology, collaborating with international teams to build scalable data solutions and drive innovation.

Outside of work, I channel my competitive spirit into strength training and Brazilian jiu-jitsu. As an active competitor, I’ve earned recognition as a finalist and medalist. I believe in the discipline of continuous improvement, whether in the gym, on the mat, or in business.
